Sailing is Freedom

These days, life is busy. Hectic. And it can sometimes feel like a cage.

Sailing gives you the opportunity to break free from the daily struggle by getting you out into nature and palling around with friends. But sailing has some high barriers to entry: Cost is the main one, and then there's maintenance, training, and experience.

Co-operatives ease these hurdles by allowing groups of people to pool their resources and share boats as they gain experience with sailing and exposure to the day-to-day realities of boat ownership. Usually these organizations are non-profits and rely on membership dues and "Sweat Equity" to maintain their fleet.

Pomodoro-Compatible

The well-known Pomodoro technique is useful for tackling a fixed list of tasks.

  • 1 Pomodoro: 25 minutes plus a 5-minute rest
  • After 2 Pomodoros, a longer 15-minute rest
  • Before starting the day, map out a list of your tasks
  • Each Pomodoro is for focusing on 1 task
